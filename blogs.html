Heart Failure (HF)

Heart Failure (HF) is a condition where the heart cannot pump blood effectively to other parts of the body. It is of two main types: Heart Failure with Reduced Ejection Fraction (HFrEF): The heart cannot pump blood effectively. Heart Failure with Preserved Ejection Fraction (HFpEF): The heart cannot relax properly, affecting blood filling.
