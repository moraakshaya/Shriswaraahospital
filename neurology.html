Neurology

Expert Neurological Care

At Shri Swaraa MultiSpeciality Hospital, our Neurology Department is dedicated to diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. From headaches and seizures to complex neurological diseases, our neurologists provide comprehensive, compassionate care for patients of all ages.

Why Choose Our Neurology Department?

Our Neurology Department is led by experienced neurologists equipped with advanced diagnostic tools like EEG, EMG, and MRI. We specialize in treating stroke, epilepsy, migraines, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and more. Our holistic approach focuses on early diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and long-term neurological rehabilitation, ensuring the best outcomes for every patient.

Our Services

Stroke Management

Emergency stroke care and post-stroke rehabilitation with modern neuroimaging support.

Epilepsy Treatment

Accurate diagnosis and long-term management of seizures and epilepsy syndromes.

Migraine & Headache Clinic

Relief from chronic headaches, migraines, and tension-type headaches through tailored therapies.

Parkinson’s & Movement Disorders

Advanced care for Parkinson’s disease, tremors, dystonia, and related disorders.

Multiple Sclerosis (MS) Care

Comprehensive treatment and support for MS patients using immunotherapy and neuro-rehab.

Neuropathy & Nerve Disorders

Evaluation and management of peripheral neuropathy, nerve pain, and motor disorders.

Vertigo & Balance Disorders

Specialist care for dizziness, vertigo, and coordination-related neurological issues.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Dedicated programs for recovery after neurological injuries and surgeries.
